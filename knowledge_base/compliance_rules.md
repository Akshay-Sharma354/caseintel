# LEGAL COMPLIANCE RULES & STANDARDS

## Data Protection Laws
- GDPR (EU): Protect personal data
- CCPA (California): Consumer privacy rights
- HIPAA (Healthcare): Protect health information
- Must redact: SSN, emails, phone numbers

## Contract Requirements
✓ Must have: termination clause
✓ Must have: payment terms
✓ Must have: liability limits
✓ Must have: jurisdiction

## Document Handling
- Attorney-client privilege: Keep communications secret
- Work product: Keep legal strategies secret
- Confidential documents: Mark and protect
- Audit trail: Record who accessed what

## Risk Assessment
- HIGH RISK: Unlimited liability, missing key terms
- MEDIUM RISK: Unclear payment, vague confidentiality
- LOW RISK: Standard terms, well-documented
