# CONTRACT TEMPLATES & STANDARDS

## Standard Contract Sections
1. **Parties**: Who is involved
2. **Recitals**: Background and purpose
3. **Terms**: Duration and conditions
4. **Obligations**: What each party must do
5. **Payment**: Compensation and terms
6. **Termination**: How to end contract
7. **Liability**: Responsibility limits
8. **Confidentiality**: Non-disclosure
9. **Dispute Resolution**: How to resolve conflicts
10. **Signatures**: Legal execution

## Red Flags in Contracts
- Unlimited liability (company takes all risk!)
- No termination clause (stuck forever!)
- Vague payment terms (when do we get paid?)
- Missing confidentiality (secrets exposed!)
- One-sided indemnification (unfair)

## Key Contract Types
- Service Agreements: Work/services to be provided
- Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDA): Keep secrets confidential
- Employment Contracts: Employee rights and duties
- Non-Compete Agreements: Can't work for competitors
- Purchase Agreements: Buying/selling goods
- Licensing Agreements: Using IP/technology
