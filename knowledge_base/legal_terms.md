# LEGAL TERMS GLOSSARY

## Contract Terms
- **Indemnification**: One party pays for losses caused by other
- **Force Majeure**: Unforeseen events preventing performance
- **Severability**: If one clause invalid, others still work
- **Arbitration**: Solve disputes outside court
- **Jurisdiction**: Which court has authority

## Case Terms
- **Plaintiff**: Person suing
- **Defendant**: Person being sued
- **Testimony**: Spoken evidence
- **Discovery**: Exchanging evidence before trial
- **Settlement**: Agreement to end dispute

## Compliance Terms
- **Audit Trail**: Record of all actions
- **Data Breach**: Unauthorized access to data
- **Privilege**: Right to keep communications secret
- **Retention**: How long to keep documents
- **Destruction**: Proper way to delete documents
